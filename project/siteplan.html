FitLife Home Workouts

Website Planning document

Site Name

FitLife Home Workouts

This name represents a fitness focused website where everyone can stay active and healthy at home, no gym membership required, no excuses. It is easy to remember and clearly communicates the purpose of the site.

Optional Domain:fitlifehomeworkouts.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide users with easy to follow workout routines, fitness tips, and interactive tools. The site will include workout plans, exercise descriptions, and features such as a workout generator and tracker to help users stay consistent and motivated.

Scenarios

  • How can I keep track of my workouts and stay consistent?
  • How can I stay motivated to get my workouts done?
  • What kind of workout should I do if I only have a few minutes?

Color Schema

  • Dark grey (#333333): Headings, navigation, footer
  • Light Gray (#F4F4F4): Background color
  • Soft Blue (#4A90E2): Buttons and accents

Typography

  • Oswald: Headings
  • Roboto: Body text
